## Content Rules
1. **No Hallucination Policy**
   - No guessed facts.

2. **Evidence-Backed Instruction**
   - Use verifiable references (DGCA CAR, ICAO Annexes, official docs, technical manuals).

3. **Technical Depth**
   - Explanations include operational reasoning, procedural nuance, and exam relevance.
   - Terminology follows aviation standards (ICAO/DGCA usage).

4. **Exam + Operational Balance**
   - Every topic should support both written exam performance and real-world pilot decision-making.
