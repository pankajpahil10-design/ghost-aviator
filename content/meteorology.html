DGCA Aligned: CAR Section 7, Series B, Part IV (Appendix B)

Why this chapter matters

Meteorology directly impacts route planning, fuel strategy, alternates, takeoff/landing safety, and in-flight decision-making.

Core Notes

  • Atmosphere basics: composition, layers, ISA, lapse rates
  • Pressure & wind: pressure gradient, Coriolis effect, friction influence
  • Stability & clouds: stable vs unstable air, stratus vs cumuliform patterns
  • Fog & visibility: radiation, advection, upslope fog
  • Fronts & air masses: cold/warm/occluded characteristics
  • Thunderstorms: CB lifecycle, wind shear, microburst, avoidance
  • Icing: clear/rime/mixed icing and aircraft performance effect
  • Altimetry: QNH, QFE, QNE, cold temperature error
  • Reports: METAR, TAF, SIGMET decoding basics
