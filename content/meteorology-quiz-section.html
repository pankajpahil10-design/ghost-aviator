MCQs

  1. Main weather-producing atmospheric layer?
    Answer: Troposphere
  2. Standard sea-level pressure?
    Answer: 1013.25 hPa
  3. Coriolis effect in Northern Hemisphere?
    Answer: Deflects wind to the right
  4. Why does surface wind cross isobars?
    Answer: Friction
  5. Unstable air usually gives which cloud family?
    Answer: Cumuliform clouds
  6. Radiation fog forms best during?
    Answer: Clear calm night
  7. Advection fog cause?
    Answer: Warm moist air over colder surface
  8. Cold front weather tendency?
    Answer: Showery weather, possible CB
  9. Most severe turbulence/hail cloud?
    Answer: Cumulonimbus
  10. Wind shear is most dangerous in which phase?
    Answer: Takeoff and landing
